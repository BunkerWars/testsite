Common AC Problems and Our Solutions

The Three Major Issues:

  • Dirt: Dirty air filters, blower wheels, and coils can severely impact your AC's efficiency.
  • Water Leaks: Leaks can cause significant damage and reduce your system's performance.
  • Freon Leaks: Low refrigerant levels can prevent your AC from cooling effectively.

Our Comprehensive Service Includes:

  • Thorough Inspection: We meticulously inspect your entire AC system.
  • Factory Refresh: We clean and tune up your system, ensuring it runs like new.
  • CleanGuard Disinfectant: Applied to indoor equipment to kill bacteria and mold, preventing bad odors.
  • Freon Level Check and Top-off: We ensure your refrigerant levels are optimal for peak performance.
